3. Voice notes, images, and files in chat
Coaches teach with their voice. Right now they can only send text. Adding voice notes alone would make Klave feel 5× more alive than Telegram for teaching. Images and PDF/video attachments come with the same upload pipeline.

4. A real "Lessons" tab inside each group
